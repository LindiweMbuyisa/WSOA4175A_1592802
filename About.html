WHO AM I ?

INTERACTIVE

DESIGNER

Lindiwe Mbuyisa is a Joburg based interactive designer with creative and technical skills specialising in interactivity, creative development and motion typography. I define myself as an interactive designer because most of my work is interactive -games, websites and other unique experiences that focus on everyday life which most of the time is influenced by my politics and societal issues. You can also find me doing motion typography that I am always apprehensive/nervous to share. Hence this website is a dedication to my typography work.
